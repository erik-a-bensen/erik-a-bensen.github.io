Before joining CMU in Fall 2023, I was a research scientist at the <a href="https://www.nrel.gov/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="bio-link">National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL)</a>, where I worked on a wide range of problems from understanding the energy costs of AI to real-time traffic state estimation.
